Who am I?

My name is Mehrdad Najapfour (مهرداد نجف‌پور). I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Laboratory of Advanced Biotechnologies for Health Assessment (LAB-HA) within the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Lassonde School of Engineering, York University, Toronto, Canada, under the supervision of Dr. Neda Salahandish.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2024 from the Université du Québec à Montréal (UQAM), Canada, under the supervision of Dr. Vestislav Apostolov and Dr. Frédéric Rochon. My mathematical genealogy can be found here: Mathematics Genealogy Project.

My research spans Geometric Deep Learning, Geometric Analysis, Partial Differential Equations, Applied Statistics, Statistical Learning, and Mathematical Biology. As an AI/ML Algorithm and Statistical Modeling Expert, I develop principled methods that bridge theoretical insight with real-world applications.

I am passionate about teaching from a rigorous mathematical perspective and take pride in helping others appreciate the discipline’s intrinsic beauty. This enthusiasm complements my strong dedication to research, with the goal of leading innovative projects at the intersection of mathematical theory, artificial intelligence, and practical implementation.

Beyond mathematics and AI, I am an amateur reader of history and literature.

I welcome opportunities to connect and collaborate.
